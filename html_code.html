Projects

Item Sorting Machine

This project involves the creation of an advanced item sorting machine, leveraging a diverse skill set in mechanical engineering. Through hand sketching and CAD proficiency (AutoCAD for 2D and Inventor for 3D), concepts will be translated into precise virtual models. Rapid prototyping using laser cutting will expedite the fabrication process. Proficiency in shop tools, machine design principles, and physics-driven analysis will ensure the robustness and efficiency of the system. Effective teamwork, project management, and risk reduction strategies will be employed throughout the project. Additionally, strong communication skills in both oral presentations and written reports will play a pivotal role in conveying complex engineering concepts. The goal is to contribute to the advancement of item sorting technology, with potential applications in various industries.
